All page categories are declining — SEO audit needed

Every top page shows negative growth vs prior period (homepage −21.8%, Contact −33.6%, About −74%). A technical SEO audit and content refresh for the top 5 pages should be prioritised immediately.

Homepage absorbs 52% of all views — improve depth

849 of ~1,620 total views go to the homepage. Visitors aren't going deeper. Add clear CTAs on the homepage (Contact, Services, Blog) and improve internal linking to increase pages-per-session.

USA leads website but LinkedIn is India-heavy

Website: USA 396 users. LinkedIn: Bengaluru 16.9% of followers. This geographic mismatch suggests your website targets US SAP buyers while LinkedIn builds an India network. Align both strategies with market-specific content.

Direct traffic at 61.6% shows strong brand recall

841 direct sessions means people know the Noshtek URL. Build on this brand equity with an email newsletter and retargeting campaigns to keep the audience engaged and returning.

🔍

Contact page: 143 views but 0 key events tracked
